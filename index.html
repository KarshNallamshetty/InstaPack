Introducing InstaPack:

InstaPack is a reusable ice pack-like a medical device that is used to help athletes recover from their injuries. The function of the device is to be a pack that quickly can change from hot to cold, hence the name. You probably have seen your favorite player get injured and wished that they could recover quicker. If you have played a sport before, you also probably know that you should ice the injury and also heat it up. Because InstaPack is a two in one device, it can be used for both causes. InstaPack is also reusable, so can keep using it for as long as you like.

InstaPack is sponsored by many athletes and organizations. We have received sponsorships from Nike, Usain Bolt, Michael Phelps, and others. Right now, around 30,000 clients use InstaPacks. Our product also sells in many big sports stores throughout the country, such as Sports Basement and Big 5. InstaPack is currently selling for 13$ a pack online and 15$ in stores. Instructions:

Before every use, add water into the top of the bag and fill to the the line in the bag. Then, select the mode and let it sit for one minute. After that, you can apply it to the area of need and can change the temperature freely.
